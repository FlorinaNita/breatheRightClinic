About us

The Breathe Right Clinic are a team of highly experienced Specialist Physiotherapists committed to providing the best possible treatment and care for their patients through "evidence-based" assessment, treatment and advice.

Our practitioners have years of experience in treating thousands of patients with varied medical problems. We are connected to the finest medical and health care professionals for appropriate referrals and links to speedy referrals to private MRI, X-ray, and other scans. So rest assured you are in safe hands, no time-wasting, just best treatment, less downtime, most effective and faster healing.

Our clinics offer unique multidisciplinary team and latest medical-health technologies. The team includes Doctors / specialists / fitness consultants / Physiotherapists, complimentary health professionals and many more. An interactive team with private referrals to scans such as MRI, blood tests and referrals to top New York consultants, all under one umbrella with one aim! The aim is to work together for the best treatment option for you.
